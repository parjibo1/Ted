Our Vision And Mission

TAGCO is here to provide relevant need-sensitive services and goods in Liberia and its environment. Our vision is to be a recognized and stable entity that is interested in providing functional and balanced life services with quality for the public and high returns on investment to relevant partners. We believe that our services will be impactful to the lives of consumers, clients, and the general public. This network always provides sustainable and improvement plans to provide quality services in Engineering and Construction, Real Estate, Water factories, Waste Management Services, Research and Development, Oil and Gas Product supply, Growing and Supply of Agricultural Products, Transportation Services, Logistical Services, Educational Institutions, Health Facilities, etc.

Wonseh mulbah

Wonseh Mulbah Group President & Chief Executive Wonseh Mulbah is the founder and president/chief executive of the Ted & Associates Group of Companies which is a network of a group of companies that provide need-sensitive services to clients/customers. This network started with Engineering and Construction which is still in business now. The network hopes to establish other subgroups in addition to the one that is currently active with more funding and investors being involved. This is the motivation of the CEO “A Pastor called Bishop Dr. David G. W. Badio was advising his son and out of curiosity, I stopped by to know the conversation. Through that, he inspired me to believe in myself and the importance of group contribution. This inspiration ignited a passion to start my company despite the challenges at the time” said Mr. Mulbah. He invested a lot of thought into this dream company since he was a student in the Department of Civil Engineering, University of Liberia. When he was a senior student during his undergraduate studies, he gathered other like-minded young and innovative individuals to start up the company in January 2020. The company was established to provide a reliable brand that people can trust and through said quality and effective service will be able to build a company of national, regional, and global relevance.
